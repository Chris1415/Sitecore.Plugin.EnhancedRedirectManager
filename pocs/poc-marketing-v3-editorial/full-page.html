A note on the work

Each entry below is a redirect map — a small piece of editorial work performed against the web of an XM Cloud tenant. They are arranged chronologically, most recent first, and may be opened, amended, retired.

There is a particular kind of patience required to maintain a redirect map. The pages move; the URLs do not; or rather, they must be persuaded to follow. What you read here is the record of that persuasion — each map a small letter from one URL to the next, asking the reader to come along.

On 301 versus 302

The difference is small, in characters; large, in meaning. One says here, forever. The other says here, for now.

We have, in this office, a strong preference for the 301. It speaks plainly. It tells the browser to update its records and not return; it tells the search engine to transfer its accumulated favor; it tells the visitor nothing at all, which is exactly the point. The 302 is for hesitation — for the promotion you will end, the page you will revive, the season that will pass.

Choose your verb tense, then, with care. Most of what looks like indecision in the redirect manager turns out, on inspection, to be indecision in the editorial mind that commissioned it. The redirect is merely the receipt.
